Volunteer Work

I love volunteering as a way to use my knowledge and position to uplift my community. My favorite events are teaching girls about code: I've taught Hour of Code modules, used micro:bit devices to give an introduction to hardware through software, and always accentuate my lessons with a history of women in tech. I also design and build websites and promotional materials for social causes. My volunteerism has allowed me to live my passion for coding and design.

Indivisible - Little Rock & Central Arkansas

October 2020 - Present

I designed direct mail postcards sent to 10,000 voters, with the goal of highlighting their state lawmaker's voting record in the most recent legislative session. I collaborated with local and national stakeholders to determine which districts to engage, and performed A/B testing to determine the most effective design.

Check out evolution of the Summer 2021 postcard design on Figma.

Tasha For Greenburgh campaign

October 2020 - January 2021

I collaborated with the candidate, campaign staff, and other key stakeholders to design for this mayoral campaign in upstate New York. I designed yard signs, letterhead, and event flyers for all social media platforms, as well as developing a style guide to share with my fellow volunteers. I also designed and built the ActBlue donations page and SquareSpace site for the campaign.

Check out select designs on Figma.

GiveCamp Northwest Arkansas

October 2018

I volunteered as a software developer and helped develop and implement a cohesive contact form for the Arkansas Crisis Center that would compile results into a single source. This form replaced their previous contact form which just linked to a popular email newsletter service, and allows the client to filter the results, add other information to the form, and export the data. This will majorly cut down the physical paperwork the client was doing, and helps serve as a database of donors and various types of volunteers.
